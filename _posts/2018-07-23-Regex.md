1. When you want to replace part of matching pattern
```python
import re
a = 'aaabbccc'
#replace the bb in the center to dd
re.sub('(a+)bb(c+)', '\\1dd\\2', a)

#replace url in text
dd = re.compile('(http|https):\/\/[a-zA-Z0-9.?\/&=:+.]*', re.S)
dd.sub("", a)

```
